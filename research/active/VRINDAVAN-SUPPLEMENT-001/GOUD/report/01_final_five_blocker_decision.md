Secondary existence evidence cannot close this institutional blocker.

Object-level daily operational management therefore remains NOT_ESTABLISHED.

## 5. Kusum Sarovar recurring living practice

Kusum Sarovar is supported as part of the recurring Govardhan parikrama context. Recurring pilgrim passage on a wider circuit is not equivalent to a distinct, fixed and institutionally organized recurring practice at the object itself. No object-level schedule, responsible practice institution or independently documented recurring ritual was established. The specific blocker remains NOT_ESTABLISHED.
