# 05 — Living practice

Katyayani Trust's current institutional site offers named seva and pooja forms and states that donations support pilgrims, temple upkeep and the ashram. This confirms continuing institution-carried practice, without establishing general public opening hours.

Neem Karoli Baba Ashram Vrindavan remains a lineage and samadhi place, but a current official recurring-practice schedule was not established.

Govardhan Parikrama remains a continuing ritual circuit. Kusum Sarovar's presence within that circuit supports repeated pilgrim passage and sacred-context use. It does not independently prove a separately organized recurring rite at Kusum Sarovar itself.

Event reporting for Shri Krishna Janmabhoomi confirms active worship and festivals, while also showing that temporary security and traffic controls can materially affect access.
