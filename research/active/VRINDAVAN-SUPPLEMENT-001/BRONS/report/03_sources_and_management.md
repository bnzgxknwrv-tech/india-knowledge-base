# 03 — Sources and management

Katyayani Trust is a direct institutional source for its own present address, temple history, lineage account and current seva/pooja offerings. It supports current institutional identity and living institutional practice, but not an independent historical or supernatural claim.

UPBTVP is a Government of Uttar Pradesh statutory body constituted for Braj heritage planning, preservation, development, coordination and monitoring. Its current website identifies Kusum Sarovar and multiple district projects. This establishes a public regional responsibility layer. It does not prove that UPBTVP is the sole daily operator of Radha Kund, Shyam Kund, Govardhan Hill, Govardhan Parikrama or Kusum Sarovar.

Recent reputable reporting establishes event-specific security and traffic controls at Mathura/Vrindavan religious sites. Such controls demonstrate that access can change by event and security condition, not what the ordinary current opening regime is.

No acceptable official source was obtained that identifies the current NKB Vrindavan operator and publishes both recurring practice and visitor rules.
