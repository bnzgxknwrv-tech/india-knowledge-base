# 02 — Overlays, boundaries and visitability

## Overlay verification

- KRIYA_BABAJI: KP-01 CONFIRMED only as an institutionally carried lineage relationship through Keshavanand and Lahiri Mahasaya. No independent historical claim of Babaji's physical presence is made.
- AOAY: KP-01 remains POSSIBLE. The Keshavanand/Vrindavan relation was found in secondary references to Autobiography of a Yogi, but ZILVER did not obtain both a directly controlled edition-level passage and separate proof that its described Vrindavan location is the same present Katyayani Peeth footprint. The required double verification therefore remains incomplete.
- KRISHNA: KJ-01, RK-01, SK-01, GH-01 and GP-01 remain CONFIRMED as living tradition relationships. KS-01 remains POSSIBLE because object-level living-practice evidence is incomplete.
- NKB_RAM_DASS: NKB-01 CONFIRMED as a lineage relationship.
- SHAKTA: KP-01 CONFIRMED.
- ANANDAMAYI, RAMAKRISHNA_VIVEKANANDA and BUDDHIST: NOT_APPLICABLE to the established candidate set in this bounded run.

## Boundary controls

1. Katyayani Peeth, Uma Shakti Peeth and Keshav Ashram are aliases or related names requiring source-specific use; historical residence references are not presumed to identify the exact current footprint.
2. Neem Karoli Baba Ashram and the nested samadhi remain one candidate complex because separate management or access evidence was not established.
3. Shri Krishna Janmasthan Seva Sansthan is separated from advocacy and litigation trusts with similar names.
4. Radha Kund and Shyam Kund remain separate candidates.
5. Govardhan Hill and Govardhan Parikrama remain separate candidates: landscape versus ritual route.

## Visitability control

Public or devotional use was found for several objects, but no candidate received a current-access claim merely from existence, map presence or tourism reporting. Where exact current access, restrictions or hours were not independently established, the mandatory wording is retained: Fysieke plek bevestigd; actuele bezoekbaarheid niet betrouwbaar vastgesteld.
