## Boundary and alias controls

1. Katyayani Peeth, Uma Shakti Peeth and Keshav Ashram are source-dependent aliases or related names. A historical residence reference is not presumed to identify the exact present footprint.
2. Neem Karoli Baba Ashram and its nested samadhi remain one candidate complex because independent management or access evidence for a separate candidate was not established.
3. Shri Krishna Janmasthan Seva Sansthan is separated from similarly named advocacy and litigation trusts.
4. Radha Kund and Shyam Kund remain two adjoining candidates because both physical names and ritual identities are distinct.
5. Govardhan Hill and Govardhan Parikrama remain separate: sacred landscape versus ritual route.
6. Kusum Sarovar remains a separate object on the wider parikrama circuit and is not merged into the route.

## Management and visitability

Public authorities, temple institutions and current reporting support the existence or continuing devotional use of several candidates, but they do not establish a single operational manager or a stable current access regime for every object. District administration, local bodies, temple organizations and the Uttar Pradesh Braj Teerth Vikas Parishad may each have different responsibilities; no unsupported consolidation into one manager is made.

No candidate receives an exact current-access claim from map presence, generic travel pages, general public use or festival reporting. Where opening hours, restrictions, temporary controls or entry conditions were not independently established, the required wording is used: Fysieke plek bevestigd; actuele bezoekbaarheid niet betrouwbaar vastgesteld.
