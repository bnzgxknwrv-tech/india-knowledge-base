## KP-01 — Katyayani Peeth, Vrindavan
Place types: LINEAGE, LIVING_PRACTICE, EVENT (tradition-bound).
Existing status: A; not reassessed.
Physical/institutional identity: current temple-ashram complex represented by Katyayani Trust. The official lineage page identifies Swami Keshavanand as connected to Kamrup Math and as a disciple of Lahiri Mahasaya, and states that he consecrated the Katyayani temple at the Vrindavan Shakti Peeth in 1923.
Tradition/lineage: Shakta identity plus institutionally asserted Kriya lineage through Lahiri Mahasaya. Any Babaji relation remains an own-lineage tradition claim, not independent history.
Living practice: the official site exposes temple, seva/pooja, events and ashram sections.
Visitability: Fysieke plek bevestigd; actuele bezoekbaarheid niet betrouwbaar vastgesteld.
Overlays: KRIYA_BABAJI CONFIRMED as lineage relation; SHAKTA CONFIRMED; AOAY POSSIBLE pending direct edition-level double verification.
