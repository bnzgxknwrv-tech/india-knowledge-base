# 04 — Uncertainties, aliases and boundaries

1. Katyayani Peeth / Uma Shakti Peeth / Keshav Ashram naming requires careful alias handling. This report uses Katyayani Peeth for the current temple-ashram complex and does not assume every historical Keshavanand residence reference denotes the identical footprint.
2. Neem Karoli Baba Ashram Vrindavan and Neem Karoli Baba's samadhi are treated as one complex with a nested samadhi object; a later phase may split them if distinct access or management evidence warrants it.
3. Shri Krishna Janmabhoomi, Shri Krishna Janmasthan and similarly named litigation trusts are not automatically the same institution. Site, managing body and advocacy litigants must remain distinct.
4. Radha Kund and Shyam Kund are adjacent and ritually paired, but retained as separate physical candidates.
5. Govardhan Hill and Govardhan Parikrama are separate: landscape versus ritual route.
6. Precise current access, opening times, restrictions and managers remain unverified for most candidates.
7. Ancient sacred-event claims are tradition claims unless separately supported as historical claims.
