# 05 — Ashrams and living practice

Katyayani Peeth has the strongest institutionally documented combination of current temple/ashram identity, seva/pooja structure and lineage material. The official source is suitable for institutional identity, own history and lineage claims, but not as independent proof of ancient or supernatural events.

Neem Karoli Baba Ashram Vrindavan is a living lineage place and contains the samadhi associated with Maharaj-ji. Current detailed practice, management and visitor rules were not sufficiently documented by a primary institutional source in this sweep.

Shri Krishna Janmabhoomi, Radha Kund, Shyam Kund, Govardhan Hill and Govardhan Parikrama all remain embedded in active Krishna/Braj pilgrimage practice. The evidence is stronger for broad continuity than for a single managing institution or exact current access conditions.

Kusum Sarovar was retained as an outside-list physical candidate because a current government Braj body recognizes the object. Its precise living-practice and tradition layers remain incomplete.

No unsupported overnight-stay, retreat, residency or accommodation claim is made for any ashram.
